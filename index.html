Assasin's Creed Origins

Assassin's Creed Origins is a 2017 action role-playing video game developed and published by Ubisoft. It is the tenth installment in the Assassin's Creed series and is set in Egypt during the end of the Ptolemaic period. The game follows the story of Bayek of Siwa, a Medjay, and his wife Aya as they seek revenge for the murder of their son. It explores the origins of the conflict between the Hidden Ones (predecessors of the Assassin Brotherhood) and the Order of the Ancients (predecessors of the Templar Order). The game also includes a framing story set in the 21st century, where Layla Hassan relives Bayek and Aya's memories using an Animus device.

Elden Ring

Elden Ring is a 2022 action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware. It was directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki with worldbuilding provided by fantasy writer George R. R. Martin. It was released for PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, Microsoft Windows, Xbox One, and Xbox Series X/S on February 25 by FromSoftware in Japan and Bandai Namco Entertainment internationally. Players control a customizable player character who is on a quest to repair the Elden Ring and become the new Elden Lord.

Forza Horizon 4

Forza Horizon 4 is a 2018 racing video game developed by Playground Games and published by Microsoft Studios. It was released for Windows and Xbox One in October 2018 and later received an enhanced version for Xbox Series X/S in November 2020. Set in a fictionalized representation of Great Britain, the game introduced changing seasons and received content-expanding updates with new game modes. It is the fourth installment in the Forza Horizon series and the eleventh in the Forza series overall. A sequel, Forza Horizon 5, was released in November 2021.
